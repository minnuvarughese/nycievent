V VISION


Founded in 2019, V vision is a year old company that aims to make a difference in the field of education. Over the past 3 months, V vision has organized 2 large-scale events. One of them is the Youth Talent Icon where youth all across India showcased their talents. And the other is South India’s first ever and largest virtual aeromodelling workshop. The workshop was conducted for a period of 1.5 months and consisted of over 400 participants. The workshop ended with an interactive session with
Dr. T. K. Sundaramurthy, Mission Director, ISRO (Retd.).
V vision’s next event is an applied psychology workshop that focuses on educating the participants on therapy, social psychology, personality psychology, optimism and non-verbal communication. It invites a whole panel of speakers that are well versed on these topics namely Dr. Kannan Gireesh a child psychiatrist and founder of Live Life foundation, Dr Mini Rao an immensely qualified psychologist, Ms. Kavitha Dhanraj, a bold and bright professor in psychology and Ms. Priya Magesh, a dignified psychiatric counselor and psychotherapist. These panel of speakers are ready to educate the participants for 1 whole month and participants who sucessfully complete this workshop can receive a course completion certificate.
Let's gear our minds towards positivity and peace using this interactive workshop.
